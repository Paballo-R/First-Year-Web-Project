ABOUT US

Medicare is a proudly South African organization focusing on the sales and distribution of Medical Furniture and Hospital Equipment, formed in 1989. The business was built on medical expertise, excellent client service and the pursuit of medical and clinical innovation. Today, this desire to constantly improve and grow is a crucial part of the Medicare culture and the brand is now firmly established in the South African and African Medical supply chain. A preferred supplier to all the major private hospital groups in South Africa, the business keeps on growing through the following goals:

  • Service Excellence and Integrity
  • Clinical and Technical Product Innovation
  • Partnership with Global Manufacturers
  • Fast and Effective National Distribution
