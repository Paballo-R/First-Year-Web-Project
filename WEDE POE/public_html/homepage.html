Medicare Hospital Equipment is the leading distributor of electro-medical devices and medical consumables throughout Southern Africa. Our mission is to provide exceptional service, quality and innovative technology in the healthcare industry. Our client base spans the entire Southern African region. This is accomplished by strategic alliances in the African continent with direct representation in South Africa.

We have been successful in building a brand of service excellence and quality equipment amongst our clients, and the loyalty of our clients keeps us motivated to keep growing and improving our presence and product offering. We research the most up to date medical innovations in hospital furniture and medical equipment, to bring our clients the latest and best solution and long-term investment in their hospital or clinic.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To achieve the sales growth targets and aggressive gains in market share.
  • To grow the contracted sales team to seven field clinical sales representatives.
  • To achieve net profit in year five by containing costs and meeting sales goals.
  • To maintain 90 day customer satisfaction survey results at 90% or higher.

OUR MISSION

We provide care facilities with product solutions to help manage complications of majority of the diseases which are a threat to the public. We take pride in helping to alleviate patient suffering associated with those conditions.
